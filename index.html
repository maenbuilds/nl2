Troubleshooting

Press Fix Issues in the launcher and verify Fortnite. If you get into the lobby with no cosmetics, you may need to perform a factory reset/downgrade your version of Windows (I had this issue once, and it was the only way I fixed it). If the application immediately closes, or any other NL Hybrid error happens, be sure to disable anti-virus (even if you did, Windows may reactivate it, so check before starting). This will also sometimes fix "An error occurred". If you continue to get "An error occurred..." or crash when pressing "Fix Issues", delete One Drive. 99% of connection issues can be fixed if you use a VPN (NL Hybrid servers may be blocked via your ISP). If you are kicked for "You have been removed from the match due to the use of tools...", you do not have VCRedist installed or haven't restarted your PC after installing it.
